# The STAMP

**STAMP** stands for **Simple Token Agreement, Market Protected**. It is an investment instrument that enables founders to raise private capital before their NovaDAO ICO, with built-in market governance protections that carry forward into the public token launch.

The STAMP replaces traditional SAFE + warrant structures with a single, token-native instrument designed specifically for projects launching on NovaDAO.

---

## Why the STAMP Exists

Traditional crypto fundraising uses SAFEs (Simple Agreements for Future Equity) combined with token warrants. This creates several problems:

- **Complex cap tables** with multiple instrument types and conversion triggers
- **Misaligned incentives** between equity holders and token holders
- **Legal ambiguity** around token warrant enforcement
- **No market governance** protections for private investors

The STAMP consolidates everything into a single instrument where **tokens are the sole economic unit**. Private investors receive tokens through the same governance-protected framework that applies to public ICO participants.

<Note>
The STAMP is not required for every NovaDAO launch. It is designed for founders who need private capital before they are ready for a public ICO -- for example, to fund product development, hire a team, or establish a user base.
</Note>

---

## Who Is the STAMP For?

### New Startups

Projects that have not previously raised capital and want to bring in private investors before their public ICO. The STAMP provides a clean structure from day one with no legacy instruments to manage.

### Founders with Existing Cap Tables

Projects that have previously raised through SAFEs, convertible notes, or other instruments. The STAMP provides a migration path: existing instruments are terminated and replaced with STAMP agreements, consolidating everything into a single token-native framework.

<Warning>
For founders with existing cap tables, all prior investment instruments must be terminated and replaced by STAMPs. This is not optional -- the STAMP framework requires a clean starting point to ensure that all investors are under the same governance protections.
</Warning>

## How the STAMP Works

### For New Startups

<Steps>
  <Step title="Entity Formation">
    Create a Cayman SPC/SP entity (Segregated Portfolio Company / Segregated Portfolio). This is the standard entity structure for NovaDAO launches and provides the legal framework for token issuance and governance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="STAMP Agreement">
    The investor signs the STAMP agreement and transfers funds (typically USDC on Stellar) to the entity. The agreement specifies the token allocation, pricing terms, and market protection provisions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pre-ICO Operations">
    The founding team uses the funds for product development, hiring, and operations. During this period, no tokens have been issued yet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="NovaDAO ICO">
    The project launches its public ICO on NovaDAO. The ICO distributes 10M tokens to public participants and establishes the governance-controlled treasury.
  </Step>
  <Step title="IP and Balance Transfer">
    Remaining pre-ICO funds and all intellectual property transfer to the DAO-controlled treasury and entity. The project is now fully under market governance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Token Delivery">
    STAMP holders submit a Delivery Notice to receive their token allocation. Tokens are subject to the STAMP's vesting schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### For Existing Cap Tables

<Steps>
  <Step title="Entity Formation">
    Create the Cayman SPC/SP entity structure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Instrument Migration">
    Replace all existing investment instruments (SAFEs, convertible notes, warrants) with STAMP agreements. Prior instruments must be formally terminated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="STAMP Issuance">
    Issue STAMPs to all existing investors reflecting their prior economic arrangements. New private investors also receive STAMPs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="NovaDAO ICO and Token Delivery">
    Proceed with the public ICO. STAMP holders receive tokens per their agreements, subject to vesting.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Terms

| Term | Value |
|---|---|
| **Investor token reserve** | Capped at **20%** of total token supply |
| **Team allocation** | **10-40%** of token supply (configured per project) |
| **Token unlock schedule** | **24-month linear vesting** after Delivery Notice |
| **Entity type** | Cayman SPC/SP (Segregated Portfolio Company) |
| **Existing instruments** | Must be terminated and replaced |

## Benefits of the STAMP

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For Founders">
    - **Clean legal structure:** One instrument type, one entity, one governance framework
    - **Cost efficiency:** Simpler than managing SAFEs + warrants + equity + token agreements
    - **Clear path:** Direct transition from private capital to public ICO without restructuring
    - **Market protection:** Investors know they will have governance protections, reducing friction in fundraising
    - **Stellar-native:** STAMP token delivery and vesting are enforced by Soroban smart contracts
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="For Investors">
    - **Market governance:** Your investment is protected by the same decision market governance as public token holders
    - **Transparent allocation:** Token reserve is capped at 20% and publicly visible in the contract parameters
    - **No hidden dilution:** All issuance beyond the stated reserve requires governance approval
    - **Legal recourse:** Entity structure provides legal standing for investor claims
    - **Vesting clarity:** 24-month linear vesting is enforced by Soroban contracts, not by team discretion
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Using the STAMP

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Template">
    Access the STAMP template from the NovaDAO documentation or request it through the NovaDAO team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Consult Legal Counsel">
    Review the template with qualified legal counsel in your jurisdiction. The STAMP is a starting point -- your specific circumstances may require modifications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Terms">
    Adapt the template to your project's specific needs: token allocation percentages, vesting schedules, pricing terms, and any project-specific provisions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute and Launch">
    Sign STAMP agreements with your private investors, then proceed with the NovaDAO listing and ICO process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The STAMP template is provided as a reference document, not as legal advice. Founders and investors should consult with legal counsel in their respective jurisdictions before executing any investment agreements. Token regulations vary significantly by jurisdiction.
</Warning>
