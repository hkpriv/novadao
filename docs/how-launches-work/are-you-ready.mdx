# Are You Ready?

NovaDAO is built for early-stage crypto projects that have a working product and want to grow through community ownership. Not every project is at the right stage. This page will help you determine whether a NovaDAO launch is the right move for your project right now.

---

## Who NovaDAO Is For

NovaDAO is designed for crypto-native projects that meet these baseline criteria:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Working Product" icon="code">
    You have a functional product -- not a whitepaper, not a prototype, but something that people are using. It does not need to be feature-complete, but it needs to exist and work.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Existing Users" icon="users">
    You have an active user base, even if small. Users who have tried your product and found value in it are the foundation of a community token launch.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Crypto-Native" icon="link">
    Your project is built on or for blockchain infrastructure. NovaDAO's governance mechanisms are designed for crypto-native enterprises operating on Stellar or interacting with the Stellar ecosystem.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Limited Prior Raises" icon="hand-holding-dollar">
    You have not already raised large rounds from VCs. NovaDAO is an alternative to the traditional VC fundraising path, not a supplement to it.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
NovaDAO is not a launchpad for speculative tokens without products. If your project is pre-product and pre-user, build first and come back when you have something real to show.
</Warning>

## Pre-Launch Communication Requirements

A successful NovaDAO launch requires strong, sustained public communication. The NovaDAO community and potential investors will evaluate your project based on the quality and consistency of your communications.

### Before the ICO

You must prepare and execute at minimum:

- **1 week of public lead-up** heading into the launch date
- **Well-written articles** explaining your project, its value proposition, and why you are launching on NovaDAO
- **Visual content** including graphics, diagrams, and design assets that demonstrate professionalism
- **Active community engagement** in your project's Telegram channel and other social platforms

### After the ICO

Post-launch communication is not optional. As a NovaDAO-launched project, you commit to:

- **Monthly transparent updates** on business metrics, key performance indicators, and strategic priorities
- **Active Telegram community management** with responsive, honest communication
- **Routine business updates** tracking progress against stated goals and milestones

<Warning>
Poor communication is not just bad practice on NovaDAO -- it has real consequences. If a team goes silent or fails to provide updates, token holders can submit governance proposals to return treasury capital. Decision markets give the community the power to act, not just complain.
</Warning>

## Pre-Launch Checklist

Before you begin the listing process, confirm the following:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Product Readiness">
    Your product is live and functional. Users can interact with it today. You can demonstrate traction through usage metrics, transaction volume, or active user counts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Token Strategy">
    You have thought through your tokenomics. You understand what your minimum raise target is, what your monthly operating budget needs to be, and whether you want to configure a performance package.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Legal Preparedness">
    You have consulted with legal counsel about token issuance in your jurisdiction. You are prepared to establish a legal entity structure and transfer intellectual property to the project entity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="IP Inventory">
    You have a complete list of intellectual property assets that will be transferred: domains, code repositories, social media accounts, trademarks, and any other relevant assets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Communication Plan">
    You have a content calendar for the pre-launch period and a plan for sustained post-launch communications. You have designated team members responsible for community management.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Team Alignment">
    Your entire founding team understands and agrees to the NovaDAO model: market-governed treasury, monthly budget caps, performance-based unlocks, and transparent operations on Stellar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens If You Are Not Ready

There is no penalty for deciding you are not ready yet. NovaDAO will be here when you are. Launching before you are prepared is far worse than waiting:

- An underwhelming ICO that fails to meet the minimum raise results in full refunds and a wasted opportunity
- Poor post-launch communication erodes community trust and can trigger capital return proposals
- Insufficient product traction makes it difficult for traders to evaluate governance proposals effectively

<Tip>
If you are on the fence, focus on growing your product and user base first. A NovaDAO launch is most powerful when you already have momentum and need capital plus community ownership to accelerate.
</Tip>
